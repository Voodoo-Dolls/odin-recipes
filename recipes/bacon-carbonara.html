Bacon Carbonara

How to make Bacon Carbonara

Delicious and Easy Pasta Dish

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or as needed
  • 8 slices of bacon, diced
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 clove of garlic, diced
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. in a large pots of lightly salted water, bring to a boil and cook spaghetti for about 12 min or tender and firm to the bite.
  2. Place diced bacon in a large skillet over medium heat, stir and cook until golden brown. Save 2 spoons of bacon fat and drain bacon on a paper towel.
  3. Cook onion and garlic in the bacon fat left in the skillet and cook until fragrant.
  4. Return bacon to the skillet and add the cooked spaghetti.
  5. Turn the heat to low and slowly add beaten eggs and cheese stirring constantly.
