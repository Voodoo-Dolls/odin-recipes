Pho

How to make a bowl of pho.

Pho is a soup consisting of bone broth, rice noodles, thinly-sliced meat, and fresh garnishes.

Ingredient List

  • 4 pounds of beef soup bones
  • 8 ounce rice noodles
  • 5 slices of fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 pods star anise
  • 4 quarts water
  • 1 1/2 pounds beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup of chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 medium onion unpeeled halved
  • 1 tablespoon chopped green onion
  • 1 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 bunch thai basil
  • 4 wedges of lime
  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Preheat Oven to 425F
  3. Place beef bones on baking sheet and roast until browned, about 1 hour
  4. Place halved onions on a second baking sheet and place on another rack until blackened and soft, about 45 minutes.
  5. Transfer beef bones and onions into a large stockpot, add ginger, salt, star anise, fish sauce, 4 quarts water; bring to boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 6 - 10 hours. Strain into saucepan and set aside
  6. Place rice in room temperature water and let sit for 1 hour.
  7. Drain noodles and cook noodles in boiling water for 1 min.
  8. Bring stock to a simmer and place drained noodles into stock.
  9. Add garnishes and enjoy.
