Egg Pudding

How to make Egg Pudding

A delicious plate of egg pudding using eggs, milk, and sugar.

Ingredient

  • 10 tbsp of sugar
  • 1 1bsp of water
  • 1 1/2-tbsp hot water
  • 2 Eggs
  • 250ml of milk

Directions

  1. Coat custard cups in oil
  2. Put half the sugar in a sauce pan with the water.
  3. Let sit until golden brown
  4. Quickly add hot water in the pan
  5. Pour caramel even into the custard cups
  6. Add milk and the rest of the sugar into another saucepan
  7. beat eggs into a large bowl and avoid making air bubbles
  8. Beat hot milk mixture into eggs.
  9. Strain mixture through a fine mesh and strained mixture into custard cups
  10. Steam mixture for 20 min and let cool.
